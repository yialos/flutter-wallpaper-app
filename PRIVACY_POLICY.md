## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- **Favorites:** Stored locally on your device using Hive database
- **Collections:** Stored locally on your device
- **Settings:** Stored locally on your device
- **Cache:** Wallpaper images cached locally (max 500MB)

### Cloud Storage (Future)
- User accounts and profiles
- Synced favorites and collections
- Encrypted with industry-standard protocols

### Security Measures
- Data encryption in transit (HTTPS)
- Secure local storage
- Regular security audits
- No storage of sensitive personal information
